How to Play Monster Survivors

Getting started with Monster Survivors is simple and intuitive. Use your mouse or touch controls to navigate your character through the dangerous world. Click or tap to attack monsters, collect resources scattered throughout the environment, and interact with crafting stations.

Your primary objective is survival - eliminate monsters before they overwhelm you while simultaneously gathering materials to improve your equipment and defenses. The game automatically saves your progress, allowing you to continue your survival journey across multiple sessions.

As you progress, you'll unlock new abilities, discover better equipment, and face more formidable opponents. The key to success lies in balancing aggressive monster elimination with strategic resource collection and management.
